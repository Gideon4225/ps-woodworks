The Philosophy

Dead wood still grows in the right hands

◆ ◆ ◆

Every piece of timber has a history. A pallet that held cargo, a board from a torn-down farmhouse, a branch that fell in a storm — each one carries something. This work is about listening to that, and giving it a new voice.

Nothing is wasted here. Everything was put on this earth for a reason — sometimes you just have to find it at the bottom of a skip.

Commissions

Want something
made for you
from what remains?

◆ ◆ ◆

Every commission begins with a conversation. Dark, strange, gothic, or quietly beautiful — P.S. Woodworks will build it from material the world left behind.

Custom sculpture, furniture, wall art, and assemblage. Each piece is singular — forged from unrepeatable materials, it can only ever exist once.

Good things take time. Beautiful things take longer.
